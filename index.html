Chapter 01

How It All Began ❤️

"At that moment, it looked like just another request. But life had already started writing our story."

Sometimes the most important people enter our lives in the simplest ways.

You sent me a request on Jeevansathi on 30 October 2025. At that moment, it looked like just another request. But life had already started writing our story.

We connected on Instagram. Messages slowly became conversations. Conversations became phone calls. And before I knew it, talking to you became the favorite part of my day.

💬
My first impression

You were caring. You were mature. And most importantly — you listened.

I remember how I used to complain on weekends. Since I didn't have much work on weekends, I would get upset and tell you that you were always busy and never had enough time for me.

But instead of getting irritated, you understood what I was actually trying to say. You understood that I simply wanted more of your attention. You never made me feel demanding. You made me feel important.

Somewhere between our messages, our calls, and those little everyday conversations, you became someone I couldn't imagine my day without. And that was only the beginning.

Chapter 02

Our First Meeting

26 November 2025 ❤️
"Let me teach you how people actually go on romantic long drives."

You came from Mandla to Rajnandgaon. I picked you up around 3 PM.

We had lunch, watched a movie with the world's most uncomfortable sofas, and then went on the most memorable long drive.

Romantic music was playing. You were driving fast. And I told you...

Somewhere on that road, our story became real.

Chapter 03

The Day I Knew ❤️

"My heart had already chosen you — before my mind was ready to admit it."

People often ask when you know you've found someone special. For me, it wasn't a grand gesture. It wasn't a dramatic movie moment. It was a phone call.

One day, while we were talking, I accidentally called you "Baby." The funny thing is that I didn't even realize I had said it. But you heard it immediately. You kept asking me about it. Again and again. And I kept denying it.

"No, I didn't." · "You heard it wrong."

But deep down, I knew exactly why that word had slipped out. Because somewhere along the way, you had become more than just someone I talked to.

That tiny moment may seem insignificant to anyone else. But for me, it was the moment my heart revealed a secret before my mind was ready. I was falling in love with you.

Chapter 04

Mumbai Airport, Midnight ❤️

"Home is not always a place. Sometimes home is a person."

Of all the memories we have, this one will always be one of my favorites.

I was travelling to Bangalore. It was late at night. I had a layover in Mumbai. Most people spend airport layovers scrolling through their phones or waiting for their next flight.

But I spent those hours talking to you. For almost two and a half hours. We talked about random things. We laughed. We shared stories.

And somewhere during that conversation, I felt something I had never felt before. I didn't want to continue my journey. I didn't want to be far away. I wanted to be closer to you.

The strange thing is that we hadn't even met yet. No long drives. No photos together. No dates. No memories. And yet my heart already missed someone it had never met.

That night, sitting in Mumbai airport, I realized something. Home is not always a place. Sometimes home is a person. And somewhere during that call, you quietly became mine.
